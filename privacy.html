Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Masroofy ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Masroofy is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, budgets, and savings goals you enter. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve the app experience.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob which may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out in your device settings.

Data Storage

All your financial data (transactions, budgets, savings goals, notes) is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not transmit your personal financial data to any server.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (free version)
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases

In-App Purchases

Masroofy offers premium features through in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
